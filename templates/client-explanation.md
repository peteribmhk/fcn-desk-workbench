## Short English Explanation

This FCN idea offers a higher coupon because the underlying stocks are volatile. The investor receives coupon payments if the product terms are met, but takes worst-of downside risk. If the note does not autocall and the worst-performing stock finishes below the KI level, redemption may be linked to that stock's downside performance.

## 短中文解释

这个 FCN 想法的票息较高，主要是因为相关股票波动较大。投资者在满足产品条款的情况下收取票息，但同时承担最差表现股票的下行风险。如果产品没有提前赎回，并且到期时最差表现股票低于 KI 水平，本金赎回可能会跟随该股票的下跌表现。

## Why Coupon Is High

English:

The coupon is not free yield. It is compensation for taking equity-linked downside risk, volatility risk, and worst-of basket risk. Higher volatility and higher downside probability generally allow the issuer to offer a higher coupon.

中文:

高票息不是无风险收益。它是投资者承担股票下行风险、波动风险和最差表现篮子风险所获得的补偿。股票波动越高、下行触发概率越高，发行人通常越有能力提供较高票息。

## KI / Airbag Explanation

English:

The KI level is the downside trigger. The airbag equals 100 minus the KI level. A lower KI gives more protection but usually reduces coupon. A higher KI gives higher coupon but increases the chance that downside redemption applies. The key question is whether the extra coupon is worth the airbag sacrificed.

中文:

KI 水平是下行触发点。Airbag 等于 100 减去 KI 水平。KI 越低，保护越深，但票息通常越低。KI 越高，票息通常越高，但触发下行赎回的机会也更高。关键不是单纯追求最低 KI 或最高票息，而是判断多拿的票息是否值得牺牲相应的 airbag。

## KO Explanation

English:

KO is the early redemption trigger. If all underlyings are at or above the KO level on an observation date, the note may redeem early at par plus any applicable coupon. Early KO reduces the time the investor remains exposed, but also ends future coupon payments.

中文:

KO 是提前赎回触发点。如果在观察日所有相关股票都等于或高于 KO 水平，产品可能会提前按面值赎回，并支付适用票息。提前赎回可以缩短持有风险时间，但之后的票息也会结束。

## Risk Reminder

English:

The main risk is that the worst-performing stock falls significantly and remains below the KI level at the relevant observation point. In that case, the investor may suffer equity-linked principal loss.

中文:

主要风险是最差表现股票大幅下跌，并在相关观察点低于 KI 水平。在这种情况下，投资者可能承受与该股票表现挂钩的本金损失。
